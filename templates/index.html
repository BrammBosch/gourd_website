About

The Gourd Goru is a tool developed to help make searching PubMed articles more convenient. This is done based on keyterms and the co-occurrence

The Gourd Goru works by searching keywords in all PubMed articles and scoring the relation between keywords based on co-occurrence. A higher score indicates a stronger connection between those keywords. When adding new keywords, new articles and thus new connections will be added to the tool.
